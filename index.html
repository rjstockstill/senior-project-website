Why This Project is Necessary

In 2016 alone, nearly 13,000 kids aged 12 to 17 misused an opioid, with the same number having misused other forms of painkillers (National Survey on Drug Use and Health). The same survey found that over 80,000 people died in 2017 from overdoses, which is double the amount of car accident deaths in the same year, and more than four times the amount of gun-related deaths. Much of this misuse can be attributed to a lack of awareness of drugs.

The Medication Leaflet System team aims to create a mobile app where individuals can have easy access to information about medicine, potentially educating them on the dangers of misuse and preventing drug abuse, overdose, and missed dosage schedules.
